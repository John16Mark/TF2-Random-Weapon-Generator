Welcome to the TF2 Weapon Generator!

Select the class and slot for the weapon.
Select how many stats you want on the weapon, either by range of values or by a specific number.
You can also select how strong you want the weapon to be, also by range of values or by a specific power level.
When you are done, press the button to generate a weapon with random stats.
Try different options and you may find interesting results!

Pro-Tip: set both number of stats to 0 - 5 and set Trash - Broken on the power level for true randomness on your weapon.
